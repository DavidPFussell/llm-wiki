# Prompt Engineering

## Definition

Prompt engineering studies how instructions, demonstrations, reasoning scaffolds, and related input transformations shape LLM behavior without updating model weights.

## Current Synthesis

- Prompting often substitutes for fine-tuning in low-data or fast-iteration settings.
- Reasoning-oriented prompts such as chain-of-thought are a major organizing theme.
- Prompt-free methods remain important because they reduce prompt sensitivity and operational overhead.
