# Intent Detection

## Definition

Intent detection studies how to infer a user’s underlying communicative intent from utterances, often under few-shot, zero-shot, or open-set conditions.

## Current Synthesis

- This cluster is especially relevant to conversational systems and practical NLP interfaces.
- It overlaps with sentence embeddings, text classification, and unknown-intent handling.
