# Data Stream Clustering

## Definition

Data stream clustering studies how clustering can be performed incrementally under streaming, memory-bounded, and evolving-data conditions.

## Current Synthesis

- This concept is a natural continuation of the broader clustering cluster into online and dynamic settings.
- It may become useful for thinking about continuously updated corpora and rolling knowledge-base maintenance.
