# Scientific Discovery Agents

## Definition

Scientific discovery agents are LLM-based systems that support or automate parts of the research process, from literature interaction and analysis to experiment design, hypothesis refinement, and report generation.

## Current Synthesis

- A major organizing axis is autonomy level: tool, analyst, and scientist.
- Scientific-discovery work tends to foreground human-AI collaboration, governance, and the structure of the research lifecycle.
- This thread is especially relevant to knowledge-base workflows because it treats scientific work as an iterative process rather than one-shot question answering.
