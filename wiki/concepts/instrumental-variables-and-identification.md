# Instrumental Variables and Identification

## Definition

Instrumental variables and identification methods study how causal effects can be recovered when direct estimation is confounded, using valid instruments and structural assumptions.

## Current Synthesis

- This is one of the most important practical subareas of causal inference when randomized experiments are unavailable.
- It will likely become a central concept if the corpus deepens in economics, medicine, or policy applications.
