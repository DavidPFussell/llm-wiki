# Graph Neural Networks

## Definition

Graph neural networks are neural architectures designed to operate over graph-structured data through message passing, neighborhood aggregation, or related graph-aware computations.

## Current Synthesis

- This concept refines the broader graph representation learning area into a more specific architectural family.
- It should become a bridge between knowledge graphs, graph classification, and structured reasoning tasks.
