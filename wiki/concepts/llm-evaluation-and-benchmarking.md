# LLM Evaluation and Benchmarking

## Definition

LLM evaluation and benchmarking studies how to measure model quality, reliability, task performance, and tradeoffs across prompts, data, tools, and environments.

## Current Synthesis

- Benchmarking choices strongly shape which capabilities look impressive and which weaknesses remain hidden.
- Evaluation becomes especially important once models act as agents, use tools, or operate over long contexts.
- This concept is likely to connect benchmark papers, validation papers, and critiques of current evaluation practice.
