# Topic Modeling and Keyphrase Extraction

## Definition

Topic modeling and keyphrase extraction cover methods for surfacing document themes, salient phrases, and emergent topical structure from text collections.

## Current Synthesis

- This cluster is highly relevant to knowledge-base maintenance, corpus browsing, and auto-generated summaries.
- It naturally complements clustering, sentence embeddings, and topic coherence.
