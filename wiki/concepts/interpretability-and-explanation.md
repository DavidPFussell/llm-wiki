# Interpretability and Explanation

## Definition

Interpretability and explanation study how model decisions can be made understandable, analyzable, or decomposable for human users.

## Current Synthesis

- This concept complements causality by focusing on explanation of model behavior rather than explanation of the underlying world.
- It is likely to matter more as the repo grows toward decision-support and applied modeling.
