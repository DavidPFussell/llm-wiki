# Causal Inference

## Definition

Causal inference studies how to estimate the effects of interventions, mechanisms, and counterfactuals from observational or experimental data.

## Current Synthesis

- It adds an important epistemic layer to the wiki because it asks not just what correlates, but what causes what.
- This concept is likely to become a bridge between ML practice, scientific reasoning, and decision-making.
