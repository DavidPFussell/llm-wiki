# Autonomous Data Science Agents

## Definition

Autonomous data science agents are systems that aim to perform substantial parts of the analytics workflow, from question interpretation and data interaction to insight generation and reporting.

## Current Synthesis

- One major distinction is between fixed workflow systems and agentic systems that can adapt their process.
- Data-science agents often serve as a bridge between LLM reasoning, tool use, and domain-specific evaluation.
- This thread is likely to overlap strongly with your knowledge-base workflow because both produce durable analytical artifacts rather than only chat responses.
