# Cross-Validation and Model Selection

## Definition

Cross-validation and model selection cover procedures for estimating generalization performance, selecting hyperparameters, and comparing models using resampling or held-out evaluation schemes.

## Current Synthesis

- This is a central support concept for the entire repo because it underlies how evidence and model quality should be judged.
- It becomes especially important when applying unsupervised or weakly supervised methods to organize the wiki itself.
