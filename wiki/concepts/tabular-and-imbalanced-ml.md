# Tabular and Imbalanced ML

## Definition

Tabular and imbalanced ML covers methods for structured feature tables, skewed label distributions, and practical predictive settings where data are not naturally sequence- or graph-native.

## Current Synthesis

- This is one of the most practically important applied ML zones in the corpus.
- It complements the LLM-heavy folders by keeping attention on older but still crucial supervised-learning constraints.
