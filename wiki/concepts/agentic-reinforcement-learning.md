# Agentic Reinforcement Learning

## Definition

Agentic reinforcement learning applies RL to temporally extended, tool-using, partially observable agent behavior rather than to single-step text generation alone.

## Current Synthesis

- `The Landscape of Agentic Reinforcement Learning for LLMs` gives the broad survey view.
- `KARL` applies RL to enterprise search agents with synthetic training data and multi-task learning.
- `OpenClaw-RL` emphasizes learning from live next-state signals across conversations, terminals, GUIs, and tool-call traces.
