# Knowledge-Graph-Augmented LLMs

## Definition

Knowledge-graph-augmented LLMs integrate explicit entities, relations, or graph structure into language-model reasoning, retrieval, or generation workflows.

## Current Synthesis

- They sit between pure parametric memory and purely document-based retrieval.
- This concept is likely to become more important as the corpus expands into knowledge graphs and structured retrieval.
- It is particularly relevant to wiki compilation because graph structure is one of the main things the LLM Wiki is maintaining persistently.
