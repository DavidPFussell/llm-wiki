# Reinforcement Learning for LLMs

## Definition

Reinforcement learning for LLMs studies how sequence models can be improved through reward-driven interaction, preference signals, exploration, or verifiable objectives rather than only supervised next-token training.

## Current Synthesis

- RL is one of the main ways modern LLM systems are pushed from fluent imitation toward optimized behavior.
- The important distinctions include reward source, verifiability, search depth, and whether the policy acts only in text or in richer environments.
- This concept connects assistant post-training, reasoning optimization, and agentic RL.
