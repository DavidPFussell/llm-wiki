# Topic Coherence

## Definition

Topic coherence studies how interpretable, semantically consistent, and human-meaningful topic-model outputs are.

## Current Synthesis

- This is a support concept for topic modeling and unsupervised text organization.
- It is directly relevant to whether automatically discovered themes in the wiki are actually useful.
