# Sentence Embeddings

## Definition

Sentence embeddings study how to map sentences into dense vector representations that preserve semantic similarity, transferability, or downstream task utility.

## Current Synthesis

- This cluster connects directly to vector retrieval, intent detection, sentiment, and semantic search.
- It is one of the most reusable infrastructural layers in the remaining corpus.
