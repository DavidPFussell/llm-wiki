# Knowledge Graph Construction

## Definition

Knowledge graph construction covers extracting entities and relations from data sources, normalizing them, and assembling them into structured graph representations.

## Current Synthesis

- Construction is where much of the practical bottleneck sits for graph-based knowledge systems.
- This concept is directly relevant to LLM Wiki because the wiki is also building a structured knowledge layer, just in markdown rather than a graph database.
