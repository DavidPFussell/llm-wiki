# Representation Learning

## Definition

Representation learning studies how models discover useful internal features or embeddings that make downstream prediction, reasoning, or transfer easier.

## Current Synthesis

- It is one of the clearest bridges between classical machine learning and modern deep learning.
- This concept is broad, but it will likely serve as a useful parent page across embeddings, transfer, sequence models, and graph learning.
