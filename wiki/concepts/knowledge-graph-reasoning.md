# Knowledge Graph Reasoning

## Definition

Knowledge graph reasoning studies how agents or models infer answers, traverse relations, and compose multi-hop evidence over explicit entity-relation graphs.

## Current Synthesis

- It sits at the intersection of symbolic structure, embeddings, graph learning, and search.
- This concept is especially relevant for comparing persistent wiki structure with graph-native knowledge representations.
