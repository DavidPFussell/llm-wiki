# LLM Landscape

## Definition

The LLM landscape covers the major model families, capabilities, adaptation methods, evaluation patterns, and ecosystem trends surrounding large language models.

## Current Synthesis

- Survey papers provide the technical backbone for understanding model development, adaptation, and evaluation.
- Industry reports are useful for deployment, adoption, and enterprise framing even when they are less technically specific.
- This concept is a useful umbrella for the broader Wave 1 `LLM` folder.
