# Test-Time Reasoning

## Definition

Test-time reasoning covers methods that improve model performance through inference-time deliberation, search, memory use, tool calls, or structured thought rather than only through additional parameter updates.

## Current Synthesis

- It is a major organizing axis for modern reasoning work.
- The most interesting distinction is often whether gains come from prompt structure, explicit search, external tools, or post-training.
- This concept is especially relevant to the boundary between reasoning models and agents.
