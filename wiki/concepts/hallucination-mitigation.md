# Hallucination Mitigation

## Definition

Hallucination mitigation covers methods for reducing unsupported, fabricated, or weakly grounded model outputs through prompting, verification, retrieval, or training.

## Current Synthesis

- Verification and retrieval are two of the strongest recurring mitigation strategies in this corpus.
- The problem shows up not only in factual QA but also in planning, summarization, and agent workflows.
- This concept should become a bridge between prompting, RAG, and evaluation pages.
