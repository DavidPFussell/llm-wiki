# LLM Post-Training

## Definition

LLM post-training covers the stage after base pretraining where models are refined through instruction tuning, preference optimization, reinforcement learning, or related adaptation procedures.

## Current Synthesis

- Post-training is where many practical differences between raw base models and useful assistant-style systems emerge.
- It is one of the main bridges between prompting, reasoning quality, and deployment behavior.
- This concept will likely connect instruction tuning, RLHF-like methods, and reasoning-oriented refinement.
