# Customer Experience and Service Quality

## Definition

Customer experience and service quality study satisfaction, trust, listening behavior, empathy, and the determinants of perceived service value in human or organizational interactions.

## Current Synthesis

- This cluster broadens the repo into a more human-centered applied domain.
- It is likely to matter if the wiki later connects AI systems to customer-facing or interaction-quality questions.
