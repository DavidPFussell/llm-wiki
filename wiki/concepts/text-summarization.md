# Text Summarization

## Definition

Text summarization studies how systems compress source material into shorter outputs while preserving key meaning, evidence, and structure.

## Current Synthesis

- Summarization is central to the LLM Wiki workflow because source digestion and wiki maintenance are both summarization-heavy tasks.
- The corpus includes both older seq2seq summarization work and newer LLM-era summarization strategies.
