# Agentic Information Retrieval

## Definition

Agentic information retrieval reframes IR as a multi-step agent process in which a system reasons, acts, and manipulates information states rather than only ranking predefined candidates.

## Current Synthesis

- The key contrast is between fixed pipeline IR and recurrent agent loops.
- This concept connects strongly to retrieval, tools, planning, and multi-step research assistance.
