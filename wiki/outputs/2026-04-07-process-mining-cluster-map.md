# 2026-04-07 Process Mining Cluster Map

## Overview

The `Process Mining` folder brings event-log analysis, predictive monitoring, and workflow discovery into the corpus. It is operational, applied, and unusually relevant to any future analysis of agent traces or enterprise processes.

- **Process discovery**: [Automated Discovery of Process Models from Event Logs](../sources/automated-discovery-of-process-models-from-event-logs.md) and [Process Mining with the Heuristics Miner Algorithm](../sources/process-mining-with-the-heuristics-miner-algorithm.md) anchor the classic discovery branch.
- **Predictive monitoring**: [Predictive Business Process Monitoring with LSTM Neural Networks](../sources/predictive-business-process-monitoring-with-lstm-neural-networks.md), [Outcome-oriented Predictive Process Monitoring](../sources/outcome-oriented-predictive-process-monitoring.md), and [Review of DL for Process Prediction](../sources/review-of-dl-for-process-prediction.md) define the forecasting side.
