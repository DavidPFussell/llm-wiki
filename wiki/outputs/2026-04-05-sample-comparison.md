# Sample Comparison

## Question

What is the difference between a persistent wiki workflow and query-only retrieval?

## Answer

A persistent wiki workflow accumulates synthesis ahead of time by integrating raw sources into interlinked markdown pages. Query-only retrieval starts over from raw material on each question. The former creates durable structure and reusable outputs; the latter optimizes for ad hoc answers.
