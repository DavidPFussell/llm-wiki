# 2026-04-07 RL Cluster Map

## Overview

The `RL` folder is small but strategically important. It strengthens the reward-optimization side of the corpus and helps connect older general RL references to newer reasoning-model and agent-training papers.

## Main Threads

- **General RL grounding**: [Reinforcement Learning An Overview](../sources/reinforcement-learning-an-overview.md) and [RLbook2020](../sources/rlbook2020.md) provide broad background rather than LLM-specific mechanisms.
- **Reasoning-model optimization**: [DeepSearch](../sources/deepsearch-overcome-the-bottleneck-of-reinforcement-learning-with-verifiable-rewards-via-monte-carlo-tree-search.md) and [Nemotron-Cascade](../sources/nemotron-cascade-scaling-cascaded-reinforcement.md) anchor the modern RL-for-reasoning wave.
- **Experiential and trajectory-driven learning**: [Experiential Reinforcement Learning](../sources/experiential-reinforcement-learning.md) helps connect RL ideas back to agent improvement from interaction histories.

## Current Read

- This folder is best understood as a support cluster for both `Reasoning` and `agents`, not as an isolated topic.
- The most important distinction here is between broad RL background and LLM-specific RL regimes built around verifiable reasoning rewards or staged post-training.
- DeepSearch and Nemotron-Cascade are the clearest current entity-level anchors.
