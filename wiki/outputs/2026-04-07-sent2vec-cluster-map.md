# 2026-04-07 Sentence Embeddings Cluster Map

## Overview

The `Sent2Vec` folder is the semantic-representation layer for a lot of the remaining corpus. It covers sentence-level embeddings, evaluation, transfer, simplification, and a few historically important neighboring representation papers.

## Main Threads

- **Sentence embedding baselines and evaluation**: [A Simple but Tough to Beat Baseline for Sentence Embeddings](../sources/a-simple-but-tough-to-beat-baseline-for-sentence-embeddings.md), [Evaluation of Sentence Embeddings](../sources/evaluation-of-sentence-embeddings.md), and [How Well Sentence Embeddings Capture Meaning](../sources/how-well-sentence-embeddings-capture-meaning.md).
- **Modern encoder families**: [Sentence-BERT](../sources/sentence-bert.md) and [Universal Sentence Encoder](../sources/universal-sentence-encoder.md) are the strongest practical anchors.
- **Representation-learning lineage**: [Distributed Representations of Words and Phrases and Their Compositionality](../sources/distributed-representations-of-words-and-phrases-and-their-compositionality.md), [Distributed Repesentations of Sentences and Documents](../sources/distributed-repesentations-of-sentences-and-documents.md), and [From Word Embeddings to Document Distances](../sources/from-word-embeddings-to-document-distances.md) broaden the background.
- **Applied semantic transformation**: [Controllable Sentence Simplification](../sources/controllable-sentence-simplification.md) and [Multilingual Unsupervised Sentence Simplification](../sources/multilingual-unsupervised-sentence-simplification.md) show more task-shaped use.

## Current Read

- This cluster is infrastructural and highly reusable.
- It connects directly to intent detection, sentiment, topic coherence, vector search, and retrieval-heavy wiki tooling.
