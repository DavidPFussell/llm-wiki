# 2026-04-07 Knowledge Graphs Cluster Map

## Overview

The `Knowledge Graphs` folder is one of the most structurally relevant clusters for the LLM Wiki project. It brings together graph construction, graph embeddings, graph neural networks, semantic resources, and graph-based reasoning over explicit entities and relations, and it pairs naturally with [Structured Knowledge vs Retrieval vs Persistent Wiki Compilation](2026-04-07-structured-knowledge-vs-retrieval-vs-persistent-wiki-compilation.md).

## Main Threads

- **Knowledge graph construction and curation**: [Knowledge Graph Construction](../sources/knowledge-graph-construction.md), [Knowledge Graph Refinement](../sources/knowledge-graph-refinement.md), [OIE from the Web](../sources/oie-from-the-web.md), and [Supervised Open IE](../sources/supervised-open-ie.md) cover graph-building pipelines.
- **Graph embeddings and representation learning**: [node2vec](../sources/node2vec.md), [Knowledge Graph Embeddings](../sources/knowledge-graph-embeddings.md), [Learning Entity and Relational Embeddings for Knowledge Graph Completion](../sources/learning-entity-and-relational-embeddings-for-knowledge-graph-completion.md), and [Poincare Embeddings](../sources/poincare-embeddings-for-learning-hierarchical-representations.md) anchor the embedding-heavy thread.
- **Graph neural networks and geometric learning**: [A Comprehensive Survey on GNNs](../sources/a-comprehensive-survey-on-gnns.md), [How Powerful are Graph Neural Networks](../sources/how-powerful-are-graph-neural-networks.md), [Semi-supervised Learning with GCNs](../sources/semi-supervised-learning-with-gcns.md), and [Geometric DL](../sources/geometric-dl.md) deepen the neural graph-learning side.
- **Semantic knowledge bases and lexical resources**: [BabelNet](../sources/babelnet.md), [Yago](../sources/yago-a-core-of-semantic-knowledge-unifying-wordnet-and-wikipedia.md), [The Semantic Knowledge Graph](../sources/the-semantic-knowledge-graph.md), and [Harmelen Handbook of Knowledge Representation](../sources/harmelen-handbook-of-knowledge-representation-elsevier-2008.md) anchor the semantic-web lineage.
- **Graph reasoning and dialog/agent interfaces**: [Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning for Knowledge Graph Reasoning](../sources/hierarchical-reinforcement-learning-for-knowledge-graph-reasoning.md), [OpenDialKG](../sources/opendialkg.md), [Conversational Graph](../sources/conversational-graph.md), and [ARK-V1](../sources/ark-v1-an-llm-agent-for-knowledge-graph-question-answering-requiring-commonsense-reasoning.md) connect explicit graphs back to agents and LLMs.

## Current Read

- This folder is a strong candidate for future cross-linking with the `RAG` and `LLM Wiki` themes because it offers an explicit structured alternative to purely document-centric grounding.
- The most reusable conceptual spine is construction, representation learning, and reasoning.
- It also gives the repo a deeper non-LLM lineage for structured knowledge systems.
