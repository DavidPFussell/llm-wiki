# 2026-04-07 AI Cluster Map

## Overview

The `AI` folder is a broad foundations-and-perspective cluster. It combines scaling arguments, optimization, interpretability, language-model training, philosophical AI questions, and a few landmark systems papers.

## Main Threads

- **Foundations and strategic framing**: [A Path Towards Autonomous Machine Intelligence](../sources/a-path-towards-autonomous-machine-intelligence.md), [Reward is Enough](../sources/reward-is-enough.md), and [Melanie Mitchell - Why AI is Harder Then We Think](../sources/melanie-mitchell-why-ai-is-harder-then-we-think.md) anchor the broad AI-philosophy layer.
- **Scaling and large-model training**: [GPT-3](../sources/gpt-3.md), [Training Gopher](../sources/training-gopher.md), and [A Practical Survey on Faster and Lighter Transformers](../sources/a-practical-survey-on-faster-and-lighter-transformers.md) connect this cluster directly to the LLM wave.
- **Optimization, search, and learning principles**: [Bayesian Optimisation Superior to Random Search](../sources/bayesian-optimisation-superior-to-random-search.md), [MCTS-Survey](../sources/mcts-survey.md), and [Discovering Faster Matric Multiplication Algorithms with Reinforcement Learning](../sources/discovering-faster-matric-multiplication-algorithms-with-reinforcement-learning.md) add a more algorithmic layer.
- **Interpretability and explanatory models**: [Explaining by Removing](../sources/explaining-by-removing-a-unified-framwork-for-model-explanation.md), [Integrated-information-theory](../sources/integrated-information-theory-from-consciousness-to-its-physical-substrate.md), and [KFriston_FreeEnergy_BrainTheory](../sources/kfriston-freeenergy-braintheory.md) widen the cluster into theories of cognition and explanation.

## Current Read

- This folder is intentionally broad, but it works well as a foundations shelf that contextualizes the more specialized clusters.
- It overlaps strongly with `LLM`, `Reasoning`, and `ML`, so its main value is framing and synthesis rather than narrow method depth.
- GPT-3 and Gopher are the clearest model anchors, while Reward is Enough is the strongest conceptual anchor.
