# 2026-04-07 Data Stream Clustering Cluster Map

## Overview

The `Data Stream Clustering` folder deepens the clustering shelf into online, incremental, and drift-aware settings. It is method-heavy and extends the broader clustering area toward streaming and operational data.

## Main Threads

- **Survey and framing**: [A Survey on Data Stream Clustering and Classification](../sources/a-survey-on-data-stream-clustering-and-classification.md) and [Data Stream Clustering](../sources/data-stream-clustering.md) provide the overview layer.
- **Incremental and scalable clustering**: [BIRCH](../sources/birch.md), [BIRCH Variations](../sources/birch-variations.md), and [Efficient Data Stream Clustering with Sliding Windows Based on Locality Sensitive Hashing](../sources/efficient-data-stream-clustering-with-sliding-windows-based-on-locality-sensitive-hashing.md) anchor the streaming-efficiency side.
- **Initialization and practical optimization**: [A Comparative Study of Efficient Initialization Methods for the K-Means](../sources/a-comparative-study-of-efficient-initialization-methods-for-the-k-means.md), [FastKMeans](../sources/fastkmeans.md), and [In Search of Deterministic Methods for Initialising K-Means](../sources/in-search-of-deterministic-methods-for-initialising-k-means.md) cover pragmatic clustering setup.
- **Drift and robustness**: [Learning under Concept Drift](../sources/learning-under-concept-drift.md) and [Cluster Stability](../sources/cluster-stability.md) add the temporal-robustness layer.

## Current Read

- This cluster is a natural bridge between clustering, validation, and real-time operational analytics.
- It is also one of the better places to connect classical unsupervised methods to process mining and live behavioral monitoring.
