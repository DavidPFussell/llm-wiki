# 2026-04-07 CX Cluster Map

## Overview

The `CX` folder is a compact customer-experience and service-quality shelf. It mixes classic service-quality theory with a few more applied communication, trust, and AI-in-service sources.

## Main Threads

- **Service quality foundations**: [A Conceptual Model of Service Quality and Its Implications for Future Research](../sources/a-conceptual-model-of-service-quality-and-its-implications-for-future-research.md), [Measuring Service Quality - A Reexamination and Extension](../sources/measuring-service-quality-a-reexamination-and-extension.md), and [An Investigation into the Determinants of Customer Satisfaction](../sources/an-investigation-into-the-determinants-of-customer-satisfaction.md) anchor the classic conceptual layer.
- **Trust, empathy, and interaction quality**: [Cognitive and Affective Trust in Service Relationships](../sources/cognitive-and-affective-trust-in-service-relationships.md), [Is Empathy Effective for Customer Service? Evidence from Call Center Interactions](../sources/is-empathy-effective-for-customer-service-evidence-from-call-center-interactions.md), and [The Impact of Perceived Salesperson Listening Behavior on Relationship Outcomes](../sources/the-impact-of-perceived-salesperson-listening-behavior-on-relationship-outcomes.md) focus on human interaction quality.
- **Applied CX and communication practice**: [Interpersonal Communication - Putting Theory into Practice](../sources/interpersonal-communication-putting-theory-into-practice.md) and [The State of Customer Experience](../sources/the-state-of-customer-experience.md) make the cluster more operational.
- **AI and enterprise framing**: [The State of Enterprise AI 2025 Report](../sources/the-state-of-enterprise-ai-2025-report.md) is less canonical for CX theory but useful as a business-context bridge.

## Current Read

- This cluster is more about experience design, service quality, and interaction trust than about technical NLP.
- It could later connect to sentiment, intent detection, and process mining as a customer-operations layer.
