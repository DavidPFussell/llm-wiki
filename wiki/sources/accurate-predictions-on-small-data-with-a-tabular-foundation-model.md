# Accurate Predictions on Small Data with a Tabular Foundation Model

## Summary

This paper introduces TabPFN, a foundation-model approach for small to medium tabular datasets that outperforms strong classical baselines under the right regime. It is relevant as an example of foundation-model ideas extending beyond text.

## Key Contributions

- Tabular foundation model for small-data settings
- Strong performance and inference-speed advantages in its target regime
- Useful bridge between LLM/foundation-model thinking and tabular ML
