# Language Models are Few-Shot Learners

## Summary

This is the GPT-3 paper and a foundational reference for in-context learning. It is one of the central historical sources in the `LLM` folder.

## Key Contributions

- Establishes large-scale few-shot and in-context learning as a major paradigm
- Demonstrates strong task-agnostic transfer without gradient updates
- Important backbone reference for prompting and scaling discussions
