## Summary

This broad survey frames foundation agents through a modular, brain-inspired architecture and covers memory, world modeling, self-enhancement, multi-agent collaboration, and safety. It looks like a high-level umbrella reference for the `Agent` folder.

## Key Contributions

- Modular architecture linking agent components to cognitive analogies
- Broad review of self-enhancement and continual improvement
- Strong emphasis on multi-agent collaboration and safety
