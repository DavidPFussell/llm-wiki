# Jointly optimizing word representations for lexical and sentential tasks

## Summary

We introduce C-PHRASE, a distributional semantic model that learns word representations by optimizing context prediction for phrases at all levels in a syntactic tree, from single words to full sentences. C-PHRASE outperforms the state-of-theart C-BOW model on a variety of lexical tasks.
