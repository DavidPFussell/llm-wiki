# A Survey of Prompt Engineering Methods

## Summary

This survey organizes prompt engineering methods across many NLP tasks and datasets. Its main value is as a broad map of prompting strategies and their task-specific use rather than as a single-method contribution.

## Key Contributions

- Survey of prompting methods across many NLP tasks
- Granular grouping by task rather than only by broad application
- Useful taxonomy reference for prompting workflows
