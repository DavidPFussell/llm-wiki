# Multi-token Prediction

## Summary

This paper proposes training language models to predict multiple future tokens at once, improving sample efficiency and sometimes inference speed. It is important as a training-objective reference rather than as an application paper.

## Key Contributions

- Multi-token prediction as an auxiliary training objective
- Improved sample efficiency and stronger coding performance
- Potential relevance for reasoning and induction capabilities
