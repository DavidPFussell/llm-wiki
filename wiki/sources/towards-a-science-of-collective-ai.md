# Towards a Science of Collective AI

## Summary

This paper argues that multi-agent systems need a more rigorous science of collaboration rather than blind trial-and-error optimization. It proposes collaboration gain as a metric and advocates systematic factor attribution over MAS design choices.

## Key Contributions

- Pushes MAS research toward scientific attribution rather than ad hoc tuning
- Proposes collaboration gain as a core metric
- Useful as a conceptual bridge between orchestration and evaluation
