# LLMS GET LOST IN MULTI-TURN CONVERSATION

## Summary

, LLMs are less likely to reach a correct solution in multi-turn settings (lower P ), and when they do, the final solutions they reach are longer (bloated), hinting that the solutions are qualitatively worse. F.3 Over-adjust based on Last Turn of Conversation Because the summary task requires the assistant to attribute its summary back to documents through citation, the task offers a unique opportunity to analyze what turns of information LLMs pay attention to as the multi-turn conversation progresses.
