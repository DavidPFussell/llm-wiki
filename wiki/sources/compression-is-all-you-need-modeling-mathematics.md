# Compression is all you need: Modeling Mathematics

## Summary

This paper is somewhat peripheral to the rest of the `agents` cluster. It argues that human mathematics is distinguished by compressibility and studies that claim through formal models and the Lean MathLib dependency graph. It may still prove useful as a conceptual source if compression becomes a recurring lens for agent memory, abstraction, or knowledge organization.
