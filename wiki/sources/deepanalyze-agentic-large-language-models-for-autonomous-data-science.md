# DeepAnalyze: Agentic Large Language Models for Autonomous Data Science

## Summary

DeepAnalyze proposes an agentic LLM designed for autonomous data science, aiming to cover the full pipeline from raw data to analyst-grade reports. It explicitly contrasts itself with workflow-based agents that rely on predefined pipelines.

## Key Contributions

- End-to-end framing for autonomous data science
- Curriculum-based agentic training paradigm
- Strong emphasis on report-quality outputs rather than narrow task completion alone
