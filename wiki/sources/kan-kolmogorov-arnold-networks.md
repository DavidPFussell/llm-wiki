# KAN: Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks

## Summary

KANs are presented as an alternative to MLPs with learnable functions on edges rather than fixed activations on nodes. This paper is somewhat peripheral to the core LLM thread, but it is relevant as a model-architecture and interpretability reference.

## Key Contributions

- Alternative architecture to standard MLPs
- Emphasis on accuracy and interpretability
- Potentially useful for broader model-architecture comparisons
