# IterResearch: Rethinking Long-Horizon Agents via Markovian Workspace Reconstruction

## Summary

IterResearch reframes long-horizon research agents as Markovian systems that periodically reconstruct their workspace instead of accumulating everything in one expanding context window. It directly addresses context suffocation in long-running research agents.

## Key Contributions

- Replaces mono-context accumulation with iterative workspace reconstruction
- Connects long-horizon research to an MDP framing
- Strongly relevant to deep-research and knowledge-construction workflows
