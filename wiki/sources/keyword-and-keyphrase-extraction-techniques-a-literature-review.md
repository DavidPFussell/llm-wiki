# Keyword and Keyphrase Extraction Techniques - A Literature Review

## Summary

In this paper we present a survey of various techniques available in text mining for keyword and keyphrase extraction. Keywords and keyphrases are very useful in analyzing large amount of textual material quickly and efficiently search over the internet besides being useful for many other purposes.
