# Intelligent AI Delegation

## Summary

This paper proposes an adaptive framework for intelligent delegation across humans and AI agents. It treats delegation as more than subtask assignment by emphasizing authority, accountability, trust, risk, and role boundaries.

## Key Contributions

- Expands delegation beyond simple heuristic task decomposition
- Emphasizes accountability, trust, and dynamic adaptation
- Useful bridge between orchestration and safety/governance
