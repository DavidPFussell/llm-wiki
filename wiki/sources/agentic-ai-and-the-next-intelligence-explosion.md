# Agentic AI and the Next Intelligence Explosion

## Summary

This short essay argues against a monolithic singularity narrative and instead frames future intelligence growth as plural, social, and institutional. Its main contribution to this collection is conceptual rather than algorithmic: agentic AI should be understood through coordination structures, checks and balances, and hybrid human-AI institutions.

## Key Contributions

- Reframes intelligence scaling as social and combinatorial rather than singular
- Connects “societies of thought” to broader institutional design
- Pushes alignment discussion from dyadic RLHF toward institutional alignment
