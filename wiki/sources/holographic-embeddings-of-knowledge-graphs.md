# Holographic Embeddings of Knowledge Graphs

## Summary

Learning embeddings of entities and relations is an efï¬cient and versatile method to perform machine learning on relational data such as knowledge graphs. In this work, we propose holographic embeddings (HOLE) to learn compositional vector space representations of entire knowledge graphs. The proposed method is related to holographic models of associative memory in that it employs circular correlation to create compositional representations.
