# Representation Finetuning for LLMs

## Summary

This paper proposes ReFT methods that adapt frozen base models by intervening on hidden representations rather than weights. It is a strong efficiency-and-adaptation paper and an important contrast to LoRA-style weight edits.

## Key Contributions

- Representation-level adaptation instead of weight-level adaptation
- Strong efficiency/performance tradeoffs via LoReFT
- Useful conceptual expansion of the PEFT landscape
