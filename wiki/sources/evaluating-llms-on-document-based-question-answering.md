# Evaluating LLMs on Document Based Question Answering

## Summary

This paper evaluates LLMs on document-based QA formats that require precise answer selection and numerical extraction. It is especially relevant to retrieval-heavy or evidence-grounded workflows where exactness matters.

## Key Contributions

- Focus on exact selection and numeric extraction in document QA
- Useful caution about precision limits in document-grounded settings
- Relevant to RAG and evidence-based retrieval workflows
