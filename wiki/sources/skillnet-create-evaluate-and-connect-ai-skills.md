# SkillNet: Create, Evaluate, and Connect AI Skills

## Summary

SkillNet proposes an open infrastructure for accumulating, evaluating, and connecting AI skills at scale. The paper is notable for treating skills as durable, composable assets with ontology, relationships, and multi-dimensional quality evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Large-scale skill repository and ontology
- Multi-dimensional evaluation of skills
- Clear “skill graph” framing for accumulation and transfer
