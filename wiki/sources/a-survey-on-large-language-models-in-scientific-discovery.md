# A Survey on Large Language Models in Scientific Discovery

## Summary

This survey maps how LLMs are transforming scientific discovery, moving from task-specific tools toward more autonomous research agents. Its main organizing device is a three-level taxonomy of `Tool`, `Analyst`, and `Scientist`, which tracks increasing autonomy across the research lifecycle.

## Key Contributions

- Frames scientific-discovery systems by autonomy level rather than by isolated benchmark task
- Connects the scientific method to LLM-agent roles
- Highlights self-improvement, robotic automation, and governance as frontier issues
