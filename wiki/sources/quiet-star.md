# Quiet-STaR

## Summary

Quiet-STaR extends the idea of learning reasoning traces beyond explicit task-specific rationales, aiming to teach models to generate internal thoughts while modeling ordinary text. It is a major reasoning-training paper rather than just a prompting paper.

## Key Contributions

- Generalizes STaR toward token-level internal reasoning
- Improves reasoning performance without task-specific fine-tuning
- Strong reference for training models to reason more generally
