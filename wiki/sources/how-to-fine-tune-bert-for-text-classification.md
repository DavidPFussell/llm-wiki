# How to Fine Tune BERT for Text Classification

## Summary

This paper studies practical fine-tuning strategies for BERT on text classification tasks and proposes a general procedure involving continued pretraining, optional multitask learning, and target-task tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Practical guide to BERT fine-tuning
- Strong focus on low-shot, long-text, and catastrophic-forgetting issues
- Useful historical reference for adaptation workflows before modern PEFT became dominant
