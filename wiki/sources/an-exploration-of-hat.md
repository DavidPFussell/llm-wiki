# An Exploration of HAT

## Summary

This paper studies Hierarchical Attention Transformers for long documents and compares them against sparse-attention alternatives such as Longformer. It is useful for the long-context and document-modeling side of the LLM landscape.

## Key Contributions

- Empirical study of hierarchical attention for long documents
- Performance and efficiency comparison against sparse-attention models
- Useful reference for long-document classification workflows
