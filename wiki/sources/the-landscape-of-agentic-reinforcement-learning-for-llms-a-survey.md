# The Landscape of Agentic Reinforcement Learning for LLMs: A Survey

## Summary

This survey formalizes the difference between conventional LLM RL and temporally extended agentic RL, then organizes the field by capabilities and applications. It looks like a backbone reference for the RL side of this collection.

## Key Contributions

- Conceptual shift from sequence modeling to autonomous decision-making agents
- Taxonomy of capabilities such as planning, memory, tool use, and self-improvement
- Compendium of environments, benchmarks, and frameworks
