# Efficient Few-Shot Learning Without Prompts

## Summary

This paper introduces SETFIT, a prompt-free few-shot learning method based on Sentence Transformers. It is important as a counterpoint to prompt-heavy adaptation approaches.

## Key Contributions

- Prompt-free few-shot adaptation framework
- Strong efficiency and variance advantages in low-data settings
- Useful counterbalance to prompt-centric methods
