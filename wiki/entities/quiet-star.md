# Quiet-STaR

## Overview

Quiet-STaR is a reasoning-oriented training method that teaches language models to generate internal thoughts while modeling ordinary text.

## Why It Matters

It is a strong candidate canonical method because it sits at the boundary between prompting, reasoning, and training-time improvement.
