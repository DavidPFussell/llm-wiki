# Longformer

## Overview

Longformer is a long-context transformer architecture designed to scale attention to longer documents more efficiently than standard full-attention transformers.

## Why It Matters

It is a recurring reference point whenever the corpus touches long-document modeling, retrieval over extended context, or document-heavy LLM workflows.
