# Self-Instruct

## Overview

Self-Instruct is a self-generated instruction-tuning approach that uses model-produced tasks and demonstrations to expand supervision.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the cleanest bridges between prompting, synthetic data generation, and instruction-following behavior.
