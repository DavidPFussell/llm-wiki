# BabelNet

## Overview

BabelNet is a large multilingual semantic network that unifies lexical and encyclopedic knowledge across sources such as WordNet and Wikipedia.

## Why It Matters

It is a strong anchor for the part of the corpus where knowledge graphs intersect with lexical resources, ontology building, and semantic normalization.
