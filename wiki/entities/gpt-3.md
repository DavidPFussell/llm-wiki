# GPT-3

## Overview

GPT-3 is a large autoregressive language model that established few-shot and in-context learning as central LLM capabilities.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the most historically important entities in the `LLM` cluster and a recurring backbone reference for prompting, scaling, and zero/few-shot transfer.
