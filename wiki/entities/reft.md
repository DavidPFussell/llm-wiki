# ReFT

## Overview

ReFT is a family of representation-level adaptation methods that fine-tune frozen models by intervening on hidden states rather than editing weights directly.

## Why It Matters

It is an important canonical method in the adaptation-and-efficiency thread because it expands the PEFT landscape beyond LoRA-style weight edits.
