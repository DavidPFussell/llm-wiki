# OpenClaw-RL

## Overview

OpenClaw-RL is a framework for training agents from live next-state signals across many interaction settings, including chat, terminals, GUIs, and tool-use environments.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the strongest “improve through use” systems in the collection and broadens self-improvement beyond benchmark-centric loops.
