# Hyperagents

## Overview

Hyperagents is a self-improving-agent framework that integrates task agents and meta-agents into one editable program, emphasizing self-reference and metacognitive modification.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the clearest representatives of the “unified meta-agent” line in the cluster.
