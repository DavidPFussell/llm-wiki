# SkillNet

## Overview

SkillNet is an infrastructure for creating, evaluating, connecting, and organizing AI skills as durable reusable assets.

## Why It Matters

It is the clearest “skill graph” entity in the collection and helps anchor the skills-as-persistence-layer idea.
