# RankRAG

## Overview

RankRAG is a ranking-centered RAG system that emphasizes better evidence ordering and relevance selection before or during generation.

## Why It Matters

It represents the important shift from naive retrieval toward retrieval pipelines that explicitly optimize ranking quality.
