# LaMDA

## Overview

LaMDA is a dialogue-oriented large language model system associated with conversational capability, safety discussions, and early mainstream framing of chat-native LLMs.

## Why It Matters

It is a useful bridge between broad language-model scaling work and more application-facing chat systems.
