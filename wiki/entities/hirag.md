# HIRAG

## Overview

HIRAG is a hierarchical-thought and instruction-tuning flavored RAG approach that combines retrieval with more structured reasoning control.

## Why It Matters

It is a useful reference point for the branch of RAG that blends retrieval, hierarchical control, and reasoning scaffolds rather than treating retrieval as a simple pre-step.
