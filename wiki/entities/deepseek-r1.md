# DeepSeek-R1

## Overview

DeepSeek-R1 is a reasoning-focused model family associated with using reinforcement learning to elicit stronger reasoning behavior in LLMs.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the clearest anchors for the current wave of reasoning-model discussion that blends post-training, RL, and test-time deliberation.
