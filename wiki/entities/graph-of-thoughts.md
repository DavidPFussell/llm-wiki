# Graph of Thoughts

## Overview

Graph of Thoughts is a reasoning scaffold that generalizes linear chains of thought into richer graph-structured deliberation.

## Why It Matters

It is a strong anchor point for reasoning-structure design and for comparing alternative inference-time scaffolds.
