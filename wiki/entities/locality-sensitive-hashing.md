# Locality Sensitive Hashing

## Overview

Locality Sensitive Hashing is a family of approximate nearest-neighbor methods for fast similarity search in high-dimensional spaces.

## Why It Matters

It is one of the foundational algorithmic anchors underneath practical vector retrieval systems.
